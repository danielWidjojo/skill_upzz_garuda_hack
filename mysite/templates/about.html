ABOUT

SkillUpzz is an AI-powered writing assistant to correct and improve English-written text. This application offers a variety of services to help overcome any difficulties faced when writing in English.

These include a translator between Indonesian and English, a thesaurus, a prompter, and constructive feedback on uploaded text. These quick and easy to use services will prevent the language barrier between Indonesian and English from realising your full potential in the workplace or in academia!
